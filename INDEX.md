## 🔍 Quick Answers

### How do I start the app?
```powershell
streamlit run app.py
# or
.\START_APP.bat
```
See: [GETTING_STARTED.md](GETTING_STARTED.md)

### How do I use quick filters?
Click the preset buttons at the top of Search & Filter tab.
See: [QUICK_REFERENCE.md](QUICK_REFERENCE.md)

### How do I customize risk detection?
Edit `RISKY_ACTIONS` in `config.py`.
See: [README.md](README.md) - Configuration section

### How do I compare time periods?
Go to "Compare Periods" tab and select date ranges.
See: [WHATS_NEW.md](WHATS_NEW.md)

### How do I upgrade from v1.0?
Follow: [MIGRATION_GUIDE.md](MIGRATION_GUIDE.md)

### What's new in v2.0?
Read: [WHATS_NEW.md](WHATS_NEW.md) or [CHANGELOG.md](CHANGELOG.md)

### Where are the keyboard shortcuts?
See: [QUICK_REFERENCE.md](QUICK_REFERENCE.md) - Keyboard Shortcuts section

### How do I export data?
Click "📥 Export CSV" button after filtering.
See: [QUICK_REFERENCE.md](QUICK_REFERENCE.md) - Export Options
